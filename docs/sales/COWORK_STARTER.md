## About Unity ERP

Unity ERP is a modular business management system built for South African manufacturing and service businesses. It handles quoting, orders, inventory, purchasing, staff time tracking, and more.

**Tech Stack:** Next.js, Supabase (PostgreSQL), React, Tailwind CSS

**Live URL:** https://unity-erp.netlify.app (or client-specific deployment)

## Module Descriptions (Brief)

### 1. Staff Time Analysis (SOLD)
Time tracking with facial recognition integration, automated payroll calculations (regular/overtime/double-time), tea break deductions per SA labor rules, daily and weekly reports, PDF exports.

### 2. Inventory & Stock Control (SOLD)
Component catalog, real-time stock levels, multi-supplier pricing, category management with bulk tools, transaction auditing, stock adjustments with reason codes, low stock alerts, Airtable import.

### 3. Quoting & Proposals
Multi-line quotes with clustering, markup calculations, cutlist integration for material costing, file attachments, PDF generation, email delivery, versioning, quote-to-order conversion.

### 4. Purchasing & Purchase Orders
Multi-line POs, Q-number tracking, approval workflow, receipt tracking with inventory integration, supplier email delivery, multi-order batch creation, follow-up tracking.

### 5. Supplier Management
Supplier master data, multi-email contacts, supplier-component mapping with pricing, price list uploads, order history, performance tracking.

### 6. Products & Bill of Materials
Product catalog, BOM with component quantities, configurable options, Bill of Labor, product costing/margins, cutlist configuration, overhead allocation.

### 7. Orders & Fulfillment
Order creation from quotes, multi-line items, status tracking, component requirements, stock picking lists, stock issuance with PDF, finished goods tracking.

### 8. Customer Management
Customer master data, contact information, address management, quote/order history, customer metrics.

### 9. Cutlist & Material Optimization
Nesting optimization for sheet materials, cut layout visualization, waste calculation, quote integration, PDF export.

### 10. User Control & Access Management
Custom roles, granular permissions (view/create/edit/delete/approve per module), activity logging across all modules, change history, organization isolation, login tracking, session management.
